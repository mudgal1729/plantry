## Recipe

1. Dry-roast the peanuts till fragrant, then rub off the skins.
2. Blend with green chilli, garlic, a little tamarind and salt to a coarse paste.
3. Loosen with water to a dipping consistency.
4. Temper mustard seeds and curry leaves in oil and pour over.
