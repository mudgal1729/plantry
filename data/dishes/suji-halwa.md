Ghee-roasted semolina cooked into a soft sweet halwa with cashews and raisins.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Cashew | 20 | g |
| Raisin | 15 | g |

## Recipe

1. Roast the semolina in ghee on low heat until golden and fragrant.
2. Fry the cashews and raisins, set a few aside for garnish.
3. Pour in hot water carefully, stirring to avoid lumps.
4. Add sugar and cardamom, cook until thick and glossy.
5. Garnish with the reserved nuts and serve warm.
