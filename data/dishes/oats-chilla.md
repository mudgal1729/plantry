Quick savoury oats and gram flour pancakes studded with onion, tomato and carrot.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Oats | 80 | g |
| Onion | 50 | g |
| Tomato | 50 | g |
| Carrot | 30 | g |
| Green Chilli | 2 | pcs |
| Coriander Leaf | 10 | g |

## Recipe

1. Blend the oats coarse, then whisk with a little gram flour, salt and water into a batter.
2. Stir in finely chopped onion, tomato, carrot, green chilli and coriander.
3. Ladle onto a hot greased tawa and spread into a round.
4. Cook both sides on medium heat until set and golden.
