Sliced onion and tomato dressed with lemon, a simple everyday lunch side.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Onion | 150 | g |
| Tomato | 150 | g |
| Lemon | 1 | pcs |
| Coriander Leaf | 10 | g |
| Green Chilli | 1 | pcs |

## Recipe

1. Slice the onion and tomato into thin rounds, slit the green chilli.
2. Squeeze over lemon juice and season with salt.
3. Scatter coriander on top and serve fresh.
