A fiery dry garlic and red chilli chutney for dosa and idli.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|

## Recipe

1. Dry-roast garlic cloves and red chillies until fragrant.
2. Grind with salt and a little oil to a coarse, dry chutney.
3. Loosen with a few drops of water only if needed.
