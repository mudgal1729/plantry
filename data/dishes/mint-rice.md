Cooked rice tossed in a fresh mint and coriander paste with a light tempering.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Mint Leaf | 30 | g |
| Coriander Leaf | 20 | g |
| Green Chilli | 2 | pcs |
| Onion | 60 | g |
| Ginger | 10 | g |
| Garlic | 10 | g |

## Recipe

1. Grind mint, coriander, green chilli, ginger and garlic to a smooth paste.
2. Saute sliced onion in oil till soft.
3. Add the green paste and cook till the raw smell goes.
4. Fold in cooked cooled rice with salt and toss gently to coat.
