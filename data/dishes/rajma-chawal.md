Comforting kidney bean curry in an onion-tomato gravy, served over rice.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Kidney Bean | 150 | g |
| Onion | 100 | g |
| Tomato | 150 | g |
| Ginger | 10 | g |
| Garlic | 10 | g |
| Green Chilli | 1 | pcs |
| Coriander Leaf | 10 | g |

## Recipe

1. Pressure cook the soaked kidney beans with salt until soft.
2. Brown onion, then add ginger garlic paste and tomato puree.
3. Cook the masala with spices till the oil separates.
4. Add the beans with their water and simmer 15 minutes.
5. Finish with coriander and serve over steamed rice.
