Spiced flatbreads of wheat and gram flour kneaded with onion, chilli and ajwain.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Chickpea | 80 | g |
| Onion | 50 | g |
| Green Chilli | 2 | pcs |
| Coriander Leaf | 10 | g |

## Recipe

1. Mix wheat flour and gram flour with chopped onion, green chilli, ajwain, coriander and salt.
2. Knead a firm dough with water and a little oil, rest 15 minutes.
3. Roll each ball into a thickish round.
4. Cook on a hot tawa with a little oil, flipping till both sides brown.
