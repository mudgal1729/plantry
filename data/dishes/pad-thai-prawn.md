Stir-fried rice noodles with prawns, egg and bean sprouts in a tangy tamarind sauce.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Rice Vermicelli | 150 | g |
| Prawn | 200 | g |
| Egg | 2 | pcs |
| Bean Sprout | 80 | g |
| Spring Onion | 40 | g |
| Peanut | 30 | g |
| Garlic | 8 | g |
| Lemon | 1 | pcs |

## Recipe

1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water till pliable, then drain.
2. Stir-fry garlic and prawns on high heat till the prawns turn pink, then push aside.
3. Scramble the eggs in the same pan, then add the drained noodles.
4. Toss with a tamarind, soy and chilli sauce till the noodles are coated and tender.
5. Stir through bean sprouts and spring onion, finish with crushed peanuts and a squeeze of lemon.
