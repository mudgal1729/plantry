Rice tossed in a tangy tomato masala with a curry-leaf and peanut tempering.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Tomato | 200 | g |
| Green Chilli | 2 | pcs |
| Curry Leaf | 5 | g |
| Peanut | 20 | g |

## Recipe

1. Cook and cool the rice so the grains stay separate.
2. Temper mustard seeds, peanuts, curry leaves and green chilli in oil.
3. Add pureed tomato with turmeric, chilli and salt, cook till thick.
4. Fold in the rice and warm through.
