One-pot basmati pulao cooked with spiced prawns, onion and whole spices.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Prawn | 300 | g |
| Onion | 100 | g |
| Tomato | 80 | g |
| Ginger | 10 | g |
| Garlic | 10 | g |
| Green Chilli | 2 | pcs |
| Mint Leaf | 10 | g |
| Coriander Leaf | 10 | g |

## Recipe

1. Marinate the prawns in turmeric, chilli and salt for ten minutes.
2. Crackle whole spices in ghee, then fry sliced onion till golden.
3. Add ginger garlic paste, green chilli and tomato, cook to a masala.
4. Toss in the prawns briefly, then add washed soaked rice with mint and coriander.
5. Pour in measured hot water, cook covered till fluffy, and rest before serving.
