Cooked rice tossed in a tangy lemon, turmeric and peanut tempering.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Lemon | 2 | pcs |
| Green Chilli | 2 | pcs |
| Curry Leaf | 5 | g |
| Peanut | 20 | g |

## Recipe

1. Cook and cool the rice so the grains stay separate.
2. Temper mustard seeds, peanuts, curry leaves and green chilli in oil.
3. Add turmeric, then fold in the rice with salt.
4. Turn off the heat and stir in the lemon juice.
