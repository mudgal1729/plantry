South Indian rice tossed in a coconut, cashew and curry-leaf tempering.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Coconut Milk | 100 | ml |
| Cashew | 20 | g |
| Curry Leaf | 5 | g |
| Green Chilli | 2 | pcs |
| Ginger | 10 | g |

## Recipe

1. Cook and cool the rice so the grains stay separate.
2. Temper mustard seeds, cashews, curry leaves, green chilli and ginger in oil.
3. Stir in the coconut milk with salt and let it warm through.
4. Fold in the rice gently and toss till coated, then serve.
