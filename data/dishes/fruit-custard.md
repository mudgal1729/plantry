Chilled vanilla custard folded with chopped seasonal fruit.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Milk | 500 | ml |

## Recipe

1. Mix custard powder with a little cold milk into a smooth slurry.
2. Bring the rest of the milk to a boil with sugar.
3. Stir in the slurry and cook till it thickens, then cool.
4. Fold in chopped seasonal fruit and chill before serving.
