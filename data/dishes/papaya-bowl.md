Cubed ripe papaya with a squeeze of lemon to eat alongside breakfast.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Papaya | 300 | g |
| Lemon | 1 | pcs |

## Recipe

1. Peel the papaya, scoop out the seeds and cut into cubes.
2. Squeeze a little lemon over and serve fresh.
