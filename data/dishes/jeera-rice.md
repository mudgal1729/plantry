Fluffy basmati rice tempered with cumin and a slit green chilli.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Green Chilli | 1 | pcs |

## Recipe

1. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 15 minutes, then drain.
2. Temper cumin seeds and a slit green chilli in ghee until fragrant.
3. Add the rice with measured water and salt, bring to a boil.
4. Cover and cook on low until the water is absorbed, then fluff.
